Create It!

Add a teaspoon of instant coffee to your cup. If you like sugar you can add it now.

Carefully pour the hot boiled water over the cup contents almost to the top. Stir to ensure coffee and sugar are fully dissolved.

Add your milk, cold from the fridge and stir.

Taste your coffee. For a stronger taste add more coffee next time, for milder add less.
